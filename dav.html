About Us

DAV College is one of the premier educational institutions dedicated to providing quality education and fostering academic excellence. Established with the vision of spreading knowledge and values, the college follows the principles of the Dayanand Anglo Vedic movement.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and ethical values so they can contribute effectively to society and achieve success in their careers.

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in education, promoting innovation, research, and holistic development.
